## Application Tips for New Sites

### Before You Apply

1. **Have at least 5–10 published articles** with 1,000+ words each ✅ (we have 16)
2. **Have a privacy policy, disclosure page, and about page** ✅ (we have all three)
3. **Make sure the site looks professional** — no broken layouts, stock placeholder text, or empty pages
4. **Add an FTC disclosure page** ✅ (disclosure.html exists) — also add inline disclosures to each article

### Application Strategies

**Easy-approval programs (apply immediately):**
- Amazon Associates: Approves almost everyone. Just need a live website.
- Awin: $1 refundable deposit. Accepts new publishers.
- Impact.com: Free signup. Apply to individual brands inside.
- FlexOffers: Accepts most new sites. Good insurance/loan inventory.
- Fiverr Affiliates: Very lenient approval.

**Moderate-approval programs (apply after 1–2 weeks of consistent content):**
- CJ Affiliate: Network approves easily, but individual merchants (TurboTax, Progressive) may review your site.
- SoFi, Discover: Want to see relevant, quality financial content.

**Hard-approval programs (apply at 10K+ monthly visitors):**
- Bankrate / CardRatings: Premium credit card affiliate programs. Wait until you have traffic.
- LendingTree: Needs volume to justify partnership.
- Even Financial: API-based; needs developer integration + traffic commitment.

## Disclosure & Compliance Requirements

### FTC Requirements (Non-Negotiable)

Every article with affiliate links MUST include:

1. **Clear disclosure near the top of the article** (before any affiliate links):
   > "This article contains affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase or sign up, Budget Smart Living may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. See our [full disclosure](/disclosure.html)."

2. **On the disclosure.html page** (already exists): Ensure it explicitly states you earn commissions from recommendations.

3. **Social media posts** linking to affiliate content need `#ad` or `#affiliate` disclosure.

### Program-Specific Rules
- **Amazon Associates:** Must clearly state "As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases." Cannot use affiliate links in emails.
- **Credit cards/financial products:** Cannot make income guarantees or misleading claims about approval odds.
- **Insurance:** Cannot guarantee savings amounts; use "may save" language.
