Our Evaluation Criteria

We evaluated each cashback app across five key factors:

  • Cashback rates: How much you actually earn per dollar spent (percentage-based or points-based)
  • Store coverage: Number of supported retailers, both online and in-store
  • Payout speed & method: How quickly you get paid and in what form (cash, gift cards, points)
  • Ease of use: Setup time, daily effort required, and app quality
  • Stacking potential: Whether the app works alongside other cashback apps and cashback credit cards

Cashback Apps vs. Coupon Apps ‚Äî What's the Difference?

Cashback apps give you money back after a purchase. You shop normally and receive a percentage of your spending as a rebate. Coupon apps reduce the price before you pay by finding and applying discount codes at checkout. The best strategy? Use both ‚Äî apply coupon codes first to lower the price, then earn cashback on the reduced total.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are cashback apps actually worth it?

Yes. The average active user earns $150-$300/year with minimal effort. Apps like Rakuten and Dosh work passively in the background. By stacking 3-4 apps, power users earn $500+/year. Since most cashback apps are free, there's virtually no downside ‚Äî you earn money back on purchases you'd make anyway.

Can you use multiple cashback apps at once?

Yes! Most cashback apps track different things ‚Äî Rakuten tracks portal clicks, Ibotta tracks receipt items, Fetch tracks any receipt, Dosh tracks linked card purchases. They don't conflict, so you can stack multiple apps on the same purchase for maximum returns.

Do cashback apps sell your data?

Most cashback apps monetize through retailer partnerships and affiliate commissions, not selling personal data. They do collect shopping behavior to personalize offers. Rakuten, Ibotta, and Fetch Rewards are transparent about their data practices. Review each app's privacy policy if this is a concern.

What's the difference between cashback apps and coupon apps?

Cashback apps give money back after a purchase (Rakuten pays 1-15% back). Coupon apps reduce the price before you pay (Honey finds discount codes at checkout). Use both: apply coupon codes first to lower the price, then earn cashback on the reduced total.

How long does it take to get cashback?

It varies by app. Ibotta credits cashback within 24-48 hours of receipt verification. Fetch Rewards points appear almost instantly. Rakuten pays quarterly (every 3 months). Dosh pays when you reach $25. Most apps wait 1-60 days to confirm cashback (to account for returns) before making it available for withdrawal.

Is Rakuten legit?

Yes. Rakuten (formerly Ebates) has been around since 1999 and has paid over $3.7 billion to members. It's owned by Rakuten Group, a publicly traded company. Rakuten has an A+ Better Business Bureau rating and 15+ million active US members. Payments arrive quarterly via check or PayPal.

Can I use cashback apps with credit card rewards?

Absolutely ‚Äî and you should! Cashback apps and credit card rewards stack on top of each other. Shop through Rakuten (3% back) + pay with a 2% cashback card = 5% total. Add Fetch receipt scanning for even more. This triple-stacking is one of the easiest ways to maximize every purchase.
