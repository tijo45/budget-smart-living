## 4. Thumbnail Design Guidelines

### Core Principles
1. **Readable at phone size** ‚Äî must be clear at 160√ó90 pixels
2. **3‚Äì5 words maximum** ‚Äî one core idea per thumbnail
3. **High contrast** ‚Äî dark backgrounds with bright text
4. **Emotional hook** ‚Äî curiosity, surprise, or aspiration
5. **Consistent branding** ‚Äî always include channel green (#2E7D32) accent

### Thumbnail Template System

| Template | Use For | Layout |
|----------|---------|--------|
| **Dollar Amount** | Investment challenges, portfolio builds | Large "$100" text + arrow + result amount. Green/gold scheme. |
| **List/Number** | "Best apps," "5 mistakes" | Bold number on left + topic text on right. White on dark. |
| **Before ‚Üí After** | Challenge results, transformations | Split screen with red (before) and green (after). |
| **Question Hook** | Educational explainers | Big "?" or provocative question text. Yellow accent. |

### Specific Rules
- **Text font:** Montserrat Black, 72‚Äì96pt equivalent, with slight text shadow/stroke for contrast
- **No faces** ‚Äî use icons, dollar signs, phone mockups, chart graphics instead
- **Color coding:** Green = positive/gains, Red = warnings/mistakes, Gold = money/premium
- **Border/frame:** Thin green (#2E7D32) border on bottom or left for brand recognition
- **A/B test** every thumbnail ‚Äî create 2 versions, swap after 48 hours based on CTR

### Tools
- **Canva Pro** ‚Äî primary thumbnail creation (templates saved as brand kit)
- **Remove.bg** ‚Äî for isolating stock photos if needed
- **TubeBuddy/VidIQ** ‚Äî CTR tracking and A/B testing

## 5. Upload Schedule Recommendation

### Frequency: 3 videos per week

| Day | Content Type | Rationale |
|-----|-------------|-----------|
| **Monday 9:00 AM EST** | Evergreen educational (pillars 1‚Äì2) | Start-of-week motivation, high search traffic |
| **Wednesday 12:00 PM EST** | Challenges / real-world content (pillar 3) | Mid-week engagement peak |
| **Friday 3:00 PM EST** | Mindset / cross-promo with blog (pillar 4) | Weekend browsing, longer watch sessions |

### Why 3/week (not more, not less)
- **Algorithm:** YouTube favors consistent, frequent uploaders. 3/week signals "active creator" without burning out quality.
- **Batch production:** Scriptwriting Mon/Tue ‚Üí Record Wed/Thu ‚Üí Edit/publish Fri‚ÄìSun for next week. Always stay 1 week ahead.
- **Sustainable pace:** At ~4 hours per faceless video (scripting through upload), 3/week = ~12 hours/week, achievable for autonomous production.

### Shorts Strategy (supplement)
- Repurpose 30‚Äì60 second clips from each long-form video as YouTube Shorts
- Post 3‚Äì5 Shorts per week (one per long video + extras from best moments)
- Shorts drive subscriber discovery; long-form drives watch hours for YPP

### Community Tab
- Post 1 poll per week (engagement signal for algorithm)
- Share blog article links with brief commentary
- Celebrate milestones with audience
