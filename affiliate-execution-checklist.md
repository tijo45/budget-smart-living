Credit card affiliate programs are the HARDEST to get into for new sites. Strategy:

### Option A: CardRatings (Recommended for new sites)
- **What:** Aggregator that accepts smaller publishers for credit card affiliate links
- **Note:** The URL https://www.cardratings.com/affiliate-program.html returned 404 â€” CardRatings may have changed their program or URL
- **Alternative:** Search for "CardRatings affiliate program" or "CardRatings publisher program" to find current signup
- **Covers:** ALL credit card placeholders (`CHASE_FREEDOM_UNLIMITED`, `CHASE_FLEX`, `CITI_DOUBLE`, `AMEX_BCE`, `SAVORONE`, `WF_ACTIVE_CASH`, `DISCOVER_IT`, Blue Cash Preferred)

### Option B: Personal Refer-a-Friend Links
- If Tijo has personal Chase, Amex, Citi, or Discover cards, use their refer-a-friend programs
- These are NOT traditional affiliate programs but can earn $50-200 per approved application
- Check each card's app or website for referral link

### Option C: Informational Links (No Commission)
- As a last resort, link directly to card issuer websites with no tracking
- Still provides value to readers, builds trust, and can be upgraded later when traffic grows
