7. **How do I switch to an online bank?** — Open the new account, set up direct deposit, move auto-pays, then close the old account after 30 days.

1. **Does refinancing student loans hurt your credit?** — Checking rates uses a soft pull (no impact). The full application is a hard pull, with minor temporary impact.

3. **Should I refinance federal student loans?** — Only if you won't need income-driven repayment, PSLF, or federal forbearance protections.
4. **What credit score do I need to refinance student loans?** — Most lenders require 650+, but 700+ gets the best rates.

6. **Can I refinance student loans with a cosigner?** — Yes, a cosigner with good credit can help you qualify or get a lower rate.
7. **How long does student loan refinancing take?** — Typically 2–4 weeks from application to loan disbursement.
